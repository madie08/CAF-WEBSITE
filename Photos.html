VERMI PROJECT

The vermicast is an experimental thesis for the 2nd year BSAB Students that was also given by Sir Eddie Candelario. And also the first year was given a vermicast project given by sir Gelmer Garcia. The project not only teaches students about sustainable practices, but also about the benefits of composting and the important role that worms play in ecosystem health.
